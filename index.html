How I earned my first VDP from CERT?

During my educational journey, my insatiable curiosity drove me to explore the query string parameters of the video courses system's URL.
My attention was piqued by the "?ID=value" parameter, prompting me to delve deeper into its significance, I began fuzzing various values in an attempt to uncover any hidden functionalities or vulnerabilities. I stumbled upon a remarkable revelation—an upload engine that lacked any file extension restrictions.
To my surprise, I also discovered that the same method allowed me to navigate through student profiles and even switch courses within the academy. Since it was my first security flaw POC, I didn't expect much.
One fateful day, curiosity got the better of me, and I decided to search my name through google, the search results revealed my name in the hall of fame! CERT had recognized my efforts and acknowledged my contribution to the security community.
The elation that coursed through me was inexplicable, knowing that my first discovery had made a tangible impact.
